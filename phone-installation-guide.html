Android automatically manages apps to save battery and storage. PWAs (web apps) like Queen Rose Hiking can be removed if not properly installed or configured. This guide ensures your app stays installed permanently.

1Proper Installation Method

Use Chrome Browser Only (not Samsung Internet, Firefox, or other browsers)

  1. Open Chrome browser on your Android device
  2. Navigate to your Queen Rose Hiking app URL
  3. Look for the "Add to Home Screen" popup at the bottom
  4. If no popup appears, tap Chrome menu (‚ãÆ) ‚Üí "Add to Home Screen"
  5. Tap "Add" when prompted
  6. The app should now appear in your app drawer (not just home screen)

‚úÖ Verify Proper Installation

After installation, check:

  • App appears in Android app drawer (swipe up from home screen)
  • App opens in full screen (no browser address bar)
  • App appears in Settings ‚Üí Apps list

2Configure Chrome Settings

Prevent Android from removing the app

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Chrome
  2. Tap "Battery" ‚Üí Select "Don't optimize"
  3. Go to Settings ‚Üí Battery ‚Üí Battery Optimization
  4. Find "Chrome" ‚Üí Set to "Don't optimize"
  5. Ensure Chrome has sufficient storage space

üì± Device-Specific Settings

Samsung Devices:

  • Settings ‚Üí Device Care ‚Üí Battery ‚Üí App Power Management
  • Add Chrome to "Never sleeping apps"
  • Disable "Put apps to sleep"

Huawei/Honor Devices:

  • Settings ‚Üí Battery ‚Üí App Launch ‚Üí Chrome
  • Set to "Manual" and enable all options

Xiaomi/MIUI Devices:

  • Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Manage Apps ‚Üí Chrome
  • Enable "Autostart"
  • Disable battery restrictions

OnePlus Devices:

  • Settings ‚Üí Battery ‚Üí Battery Optimization
  • Chrome ‚Üí "Don't optimize"

3Regular Usage Pattern

Keep the app active to prevent removal

  • Use the app at least once per week
  • Always open from home screen icon (not browser)
  • Interact with app features (view trails, check GPS, etc.)
  • Use offline features to demonstrate app value to Android

üçé Great News for iOS Users!

iOS handles PWAs much better than Android. Once properly installed, your Queen Rose Hiking app will stay installed and work reliably. iOS doesn't aggressively remove PWAs like Android does.

1Installation Using Safari

Use Safari Browser Only (not Chrome, Firefox, or other browsers)

  1. Open Safari browser on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Navigate to your Queen Rose Hiking app URL
  3. Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up)
  4. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  5. Edit the app name if desired, then tap "Add"
  6. The app icon will appear on your home screen

üì± iOS-Specific Features

  • Native app experience - Full screen, no Safari UI
  • Persistent installation - Won't disappear after restart
  • Background refresh - App stays updated
  • Notification support - Can receive notifications
  • Offline functionality - Works without internet
  • Touch gestures - Optimized for iOS touch handling

2Verify Installation

  2. Tap to open - should launch in full screen
  3. No Safari address bar or navigation buttons visible
  4. App appears in Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí iPhone Storage
  5. Swipe up from bottom shows app in app switcher

3Optional: Configure Settings
